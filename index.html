About Myself

My Lifestory

My dad’s music was the soundtrack of my early years. Whether it was the soft strumming of his guitar late at night or the lively jam sessions he hosted with his friends, there was always music in our home. He’d let me sit in on those sessions, teaching me to appreciate the magic of a well-played chord and the beauty of improvisation. Sometimes, he’d even hand me an instrument and encourage me to play along, no matter how clumsy my attempts were. Those moments weren’t just fun—they taught me to be creative, to take risks, and to enjoy the process of learning. While my dad was busy with his music, my mom was the one who kept everything running smoothly. She managed the family business with skill and precision, showing me what it meant to work hard and stay focused. She often said that my dad brought the soul to our family, while she brought the structure. Together, they made a perfect team, and I learned the importance of balancing passion with practicality.
